Scania

Headquarter: Sweden,Sales Volume: 7,777 units

Scania is a Swedish automotive company founded in 1911 and has its headquarter in Sodertalje, Sweden. According to 2019 stats, Scania has produced 7777 units of vehicles, making it one of the world’s largest bus and coaches manufacturers.

Volvo Buses

Headquarter: Sweden Market Value: 9,731 units.The company is based in Gothenburg, Sweden, and it was founded in 1968.

The Volvo bus corporation manufactures all types of heavy buses for transportation purposes. The Volvo products range from buses, coaches to chassis and different other services related to buses. Some of the famous models of Volvo buses include Volvo B8RLEA, Volvo B11R, and Volvo B7RLE.

Higer Bus

Headquarter: China Market Value: 11,412 units.Higer Bus Company Limited is a Chinese automotive company that was founded in 1998 and has its headquarter in Suzhou, China.

The company sold 11412 units in 2019, making it one of the world’s largest coaches and bus manufacturers. Higer Bus company product ranges from buses, coaches, city buses, vans, hybrid buses, and special vehicles.

MAN

Headquarter: Germany Market Value: 13,972 units.The headquarter of MAN SE is based in Munich, Germany. The company was founded in 1758.

MAN SE product range includes urban & intercity coaches, touring coaches, light and heavy-duty transport buses to fire trucks & heavy-duty trucks. In 2019 MAN sold 13972 units, making it one of the largest manufacturers of buses and coaches in the world.

Marcopolo S.A

Headquarter: Brazil Market Value: 15,831 units.Marcopolo is a Brazilian bus and coach manufacturing company and has its headquarter in Caxias do Sul, Brazil. It was founded in 1949.

The company manufactures a wide variety of buses which include intercity buses, microbuses, and touring coaches. Some of the products of Marcopolo include the Paradiso series, Multego, Torino, Boxer, Senior, Ideale 600, and Vicino, among others.

  • What are the advantages of online bus booking?

    There are many advantages of online bus ticket booking. You can choose your preferred seats. Online bus ticket booking keeps you away from the long queues of the offline ticket counters. You can view plenty of buses and choose an appropriate bus for your travel considering the amenities, reviews, ratings and bus images available. You can choose the preferred bus type (Volvo Bus, AC or Non AC) and also pickup and dropping point and timings. Your tickets can be booked at a reasonable price with the ongoing bank and e-wallet discounts.

  • Can we book state road transport corporation buses from Goibibo?

    Yes, almost all fleets of state road transport corporation buses can be booked from Goibibo. For example, Deluxe AC and Non Ac buses and Volvo bus booking

  • Is it compulsory to carry the printout of the ticket to board the bus?

    The bus operators validate e-tickets received on your e-mail, Whatsapp or available on the Goibibo app. But, it is advisable to carry the e-ticket's print out to be on a safer side for a hassle-free travel experience.

  • What if I miss the bus? Will I get a refund?

    Refund will not be initiated if you miss the bus. A refund is initiated in case of bus operator cancels the bus and have not provided any other bus as an alternate option. Please note that Goibibo will not be liable for refund as it can be permitted only by the bus operators.

  • Where can I check current offers on online bus booking via Goibibo?

    The exciting discounts and offers come with validity, hence, you must check the current offers before booking bus tickets. The exclusive bank offers, promo codes and discounts are displayed on the bus page of the website and Bus section on the Goibibo app.
